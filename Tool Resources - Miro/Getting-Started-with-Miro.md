## âœ¨ Key Features for Mental Models

### Infinite Whiteboard Canvas
- **Zoomable workspace**: Never run out of space for your ideas
- **Sticky notes**: Perfect for brainstorming and organizing thoughts
- **Drawing tools**: Sketch connections and freeform concepts

### Diagramming Tools
- **Shapes & connectors**: Build clear flowcharts and process diagrams
- **Mind mapping**: Organize hierarchical relationships visually
- **Frameworks**: Pre-built templates for concept maps, SWOT analysis, and more

### Collaboration Features
